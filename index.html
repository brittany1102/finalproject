In 2013, 22.7 million Americans needed treatment for a substance use disorder which is almost 9% of the population over the age of 12. Only about 2.5 million received such treatment at a specialty facility. It is estimated that more than 41% of treatment admissions were for alcohol abuse, 20 % of admissions were for opiate addiction treatment, and17% were for the treatment of marijuana abuse in 2009.
